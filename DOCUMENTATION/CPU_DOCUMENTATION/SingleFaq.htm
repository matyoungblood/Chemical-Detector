FAQ-11614

Print mistakes in MC68HC908GP32/H manual

Problem Categories

  • General/Other



Question:

1) On pages 53, 280, 298 in MC68HC908GP32/H rev 5, it says that the reset state of the SBSR register is $00. On pages 99 and 103 it says that it is $10.
2) In some places (pages 49, 62, 79[for IMASK1]) the bits in the INTSCR register are labeled: IRQF1, ACK1, IMASK1, and MODE1.
In other places (pages 79[for IRQF], 176, 177, 178, 179, 180 ) the same bits are labeled: IRQF, ACK, IMASK, and MODE.


Answer:

This has been corrected in last Revision: MC68HC908GP32/H rev 6
1) Only bit SBSW is used in SBSR. Its reset status is "0". Other bits of the SBSR register are reserved. Their status after reset is not specified.
2) The names of the signals are IRQF, ACK, IMASK, and MODE.
